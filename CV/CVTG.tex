\section{RESEARCH INTERESTS}
\raggedright
I am interested in macroevolutionary biology; the stories behind the history of Life and the methodologies with which it is investigated. I am particularly interested in ways of combining classical methods such as descriptive palaeontology with modern bioinformatic tools such as phylogenomics and mathematical modelling.
